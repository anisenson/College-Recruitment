Student Life

Student Success Center

The Student Success Center at James Madison University offers a dynamic, supportive environment designed to enhance student learning, well-being, and services. As a central hub, it provides a range of programs and resources to help students improve their academic efficiency, engagement, and effectiveness. The center features spaces for studying, collaboration, and learning, and it offers guidance and support for various needs, including financial, academic, technical, health (both physical and mental), and career-related concerns. Living on Campus

Our students come from both large cities and small towns, representing all corners of the United States and over 50 different countries. At JMU, students quickly feel at home within our welcoming community. This is a place where people hold doors for each other, are enthusiastic about meeting new friends, enjoy grabbing lunch between classes, and are open to exploring new fitness activities.
